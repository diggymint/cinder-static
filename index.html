LUNAR PHASES: lllThe Winter Solstice EP is Cindergarden's most recent masterpiece and marks a new era as Valentine emerges with the musical prowess to reach an expansive audience while holding steadfast to her truly personal creative vision. Lunar Phases is a stunningly crisp contrast of cynicism and dreams. Wintery tones of aural bliss dripping with satire, contempt, and a new level of sophistication. This three song EP emphasizes quality over quantity and boasts an epic display of arrangement, production, and raw vocal talent. Featuring a hauntingly angelic cover of Black Hole Sun, Cindergarden reinterprets this grunge classic into an essence which dazzles and devastates.

Additionally, one of Valentine's first true lyrical inspirations, Mr. Leonard Cohen, also receives a special homage on this EP. Our beloved songbird pouring out ethereal notes laced with a tragic sageness in her reverent delivery of 'Everybody Knows'. And lastly, the track from which the EP takes it's name: Lunar Phases, a Cindergarden original and sensual pop marvel that is destined to become an underground hit, and promises more strange brilliance to come. Lunar Phases also brings with it the first ever official Cindergarden music video, comprised of luminous cinematography by Cindergarden's longtime friend and collaborator, Marianne Williams.

Cindergarden is the official alias of solo artist Jaymie Valentine, one of the most unique vocal talents to have emerged in the last decade. Writing, producing, and recording all her own material since the first Cindergarden release in 2006, Valentine has bewitched the hearts of dark art aficionados and electronic music connoisseurs from all over this world and perhaps beyond. Signature production, innovative soundscapes, gothic-industrial club anthems, and more recent commercial success as a serious composer and sound designer for major events, theme parks, films, and television are among Miss Valentine's growing repertoire.
